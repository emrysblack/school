Revision Control
==================================================
Emacs defaults to RCS, so http://www.emacswiki.org/emacs/RevisionControlSystem
      will show you how to use it with emacs. (VERY easy).

Look at 'man rcsinfo' for detail on using RCS without emacs (for the Vi crowd).
